Purdue Graduate Student Colloquium, Fall 2026

Time: Wednesdays, 4:30–5:20 PM
Location: MATH Building, Room TBA
Organizer: Carlos Agrinsoni, Kyungtak Hong, Ruipeng Zou
We welcome speakers from all areas of mathematics. Talks may be about the speaker’s own research, a topic from a reading course, or any mathematical idea they would like to share. The seminar is meant to be informal and accessible, and speakers are encouraged to emphasize motivation, examples, and intuition. If you are interested in giving a talk, please email one of the organizers.
